Dr. Tom Peyton, Ph.D.
Founder || President & Chief Executive Officer
Dr. Peyton is native of West Lafayette, Indiana and received his BS in molecular biology and PhD in Bionucleonics from the School of Pharmacy at Purdue Uniiversity. Starting at Stanford Research Institute/Engineering Systems and General Research Corporation, Dr. Peyton applied biotechnology to natural resource recovery systems and developed biotechnology for agricultural food products in the field in Malaysia. He then attended Purdue's Krannert School of Management and was Adjunct Professor of Environmental Health & Engineering at Purdue. He founded American Technology Management Corportation (AmTech Consultants) in Washington DC that provided environmental, biotechnology and health services to over 500 multinational clients including the US Congress' biotechnology program. In 2005, Dr. Peyton patented a resource recovery food system for fermented grain and grape distilleries that extracted non-alcoholic potable water and beneficial nutrient products and founded NouvEau Inc. to commercialize the intellectual property. Between 2008-2013 he formed and operated a subsidiary NouvEau France SARL outside of Bordeaux to advance its European technology and between 2015-2017 commercialized the production of Eau de Vin "Water of Wine" from the Rhone Valley. In 2018 he invented a composition of matter and method of use for non-alcoholic grgape wine health mixtures as proprietary intellectual property (Sante' du Vin) of NouvEau with Wine Health Boost brand as a commercial packaged good as a concentrate liquid beverage enhancer, dietary supplement and ready to drink functional health beverage.
